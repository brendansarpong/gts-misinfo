Lesson 8: Final Challenge

  • You've now seen how misinformation spreads, how AI can fake reality, and how algorithms can warp your feed.
  • You've learned to think about bias, emotional manipulation, and hidden agendas.
  • In this final lesson, we're going to put it all together.
  • You'll review the biggest takeaways from the course, check in on your own confidence, and reflect on how these skills apply to the real web.
  • Because the internet isn't going to slow down or get easier. The details might become outdated, especially in regard to AI generated content, but the principles are still essential. You'll be better prepared to navigate this internet landscape. Go forth.
  • Then: You'll take a short reflective challenge that brings together everything you've learned.
