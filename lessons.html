Your Learning Journey

Follow my carefully structured curriculum to develop your digital media literacy skills. Each lesson builds upon the previous one, creating a comprehensive understanding of how to navigate the digital information landscape.

00

Intro

Why does misinformation go viral? Learn to recognize the signs and scan online content with a critical eye.
01

Sponsor Hunt

Who's paying for your content? Spot hidden sponsors, financial incentives, and the real motives behind what you see online.
02

Ragebait

Some headlines are designed to make you mad. Learn to spot emotionally manipulative content and avoid the outrage trap.
03

AI or IRL?

Can you tell the difference between AI-generated and real images? Learn the tricks to spot fakes in the age of artificial intelligence.
04

Algorithm Adventures

How do algorithms shape what you see online? Explore how your feed changes based on your clicks and interests.
05

What's Their Angle?

See how the same news event can be covered differently depending on how your feed has been algorithmically curated. Learn to spot bias and framing in headlines.
06

Substantiate the Tweet

Tweets are really easy to fake. Take it from me. Learn to spot when they're fake, or when real tweets are misleading.
07

Finale...

Review. Reflect. Pat yourself on the back. And take my survey!
