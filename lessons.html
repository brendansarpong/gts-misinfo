Your Learning Journey

Follow my carefully structured curriculum to develop your digital media literacy skills. Each lesson builds upon the previous one, creating a comprehensive understanding of how to navigate the digital information landscape.

01

What Is Misinformation, Really?

Not all wrong stuff online is a lie on purpose. This lesson breaks down the difference between misinformation, disinformation, and just plain confusion.
02

The Algorithm Doesn't Love You

Your feed isn't neutral. It's designed to keep you scrolling, not to keep you informed. We'll look at how platforms decide what you see and why that matters.
03

Follow the Money

Everyone's got a hustle, and media is no different. Learn how financial incentives shape what gets published and what doesn't.
04

That's Cap (Screenshots Lie)

Just because it looks like a tweet doesn't mean it's real. We'll break down how easy it is to fake screenshots and what to do instead of just hitting "share."
05

Media Bias Analysis

Understand different types of media bias and how they influence information presentation.
