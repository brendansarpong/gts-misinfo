Lesson 1
Spot the Spread
  • Misinformation spreads online way faster than most people realize, and it usually looks pretty convincing.
  • A tweet, TikTok, or Instagram post with a bold claim can get thousands of shares in minutes, even if it's completely false. A lot of times, all you need is a buzzy title and a picture.
  • In this curriculum, we'll look at what makes misinformation go viral, and how to recognize the signs of something shady, along with other tools to prepare you for the internet in the modern age.
  • By the end, you'll be able to scan something online and ask yourself, "Should I believe this, or even share it?" before making a move. You'll detect AI-generated content without breaking a sweat, learn how to substantiate flashy claims, and so much more. Ready?
