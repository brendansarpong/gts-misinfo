Our Methodology

Our curriculum is built upon reputable sources and aims to provide a balanced perspective on digital media literacy.

AllSides

AllSides offers balanced news coverage, highlighting perspectives from the left, center, and right to foster understanding across the political spectrum.

FactCheck.org

FactCheck.org is a nonpartisan, nonprofit organization that monitors the factual accuracy of statements by major U.S. political players.

Our Approach

We strive to present information in a bipartisan and comprehensive manner, encouraging critical thinking and informed decision-making.
