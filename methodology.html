Our Methodology

Creating a robust, trustworthy fact-checking website requires a clear, transparent methodology. Drawing inspiration from FactCheck.org, AllSides, and NewsWhip, this methodology blends rigorous research, bias awareness, and real-time monitoring to ensure accuracy, fairness, and relevance.

Topic Selection and Prioritization

  • Balanced Claim Selection: Monitor statements from political leaders, organizations, and trending public figures across the political spectrum.
  • Real-Time Trend Monitoring: Use tools like NewsWhip to identify and prioritize fast-spreading stories and misinformation.

Research and Verification

  • Source Gathering: Contact claim originators for supporting data and clarification.
  • Cross-Referencing: Compare information across multiple independent sources.
  • Transparency: Hyperlink to all primary sources within fact checks.

Bias Awareness

  • Multipartisan Review: Reviewers from across the political spectrum assess research.
  • Bias Ratings: Transparently disclose potential bias in sources and process.

Writing and Publication

  • Layered Editing: Multiple rounds of editing for clarity and accuracy.
  • Clear Verdicts: Transparent rating system for all claims.

Impact and Feedback

  • Engagement Tracking: Monitor how fact checks are shared and discussed.
  • Community Involvement: Reader submissions and feedback integration.

Continuous Improvement

  • Ongoing Training: Regular updates on research methods and trends.
  • Collaboration: Partnerships with other fact-checking organizations.
